# Contra: Shattered Soldier (2002)

PS2 run-and-gun by Konami's Bemani team. Mechanically defined by the **Hit Rate grading system**; narratively the series story peak with the Triumvirate conspiracy and rank-based multiple endings (canonized via [[Research Contra Best Story Series]]).

## Differentiated Systems

| System | Detail | Survived To |
|---|---|---|
| Hit Rate grading | Per-stage 0–100% destruction counter; D / C / B / A / S rank | Neo Contra |
| Rank-gated ending | Only S-rank unlocks true ending; lower ranks see lesser endings | Single-entry direct gate; spirit returned in score-attack culture |
| Continue penalty | Using a continue drops a letter; lives lost dock %s | Neo Contra |
| Story-driven progression | Triumvirate conspiracy + "hero is the system's tool" reversal | (Story-pillar; [[Echo Story Spine]] borrows narrative arc) |

## Reception

Metacritic 78 / EGM 9-of-10. Niche but critically validated. Often cited as the series' mechanical pinnacle by hardcore fans (Source: [[Research Contra Best Story Series]]).
