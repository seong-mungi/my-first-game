# Contra: Rogue Corps (2019)

Multi-platform run-and-gun by Toylogic / Konami. **Third 3D / non-2D perspective failure** in the series; preceded the 2024 [[Contra Operation Galuga]] 2D corrective return.

## Differentiated Systems

| System | Detail | Verdict |
|---|---|---|
| Angled top-down | Twin-stick camera; player runs/aims with two analog sticks | Critical fail (Metacritic mixed/unfavorable) |
| Character classes (4) | Kaiser, The Gentleman, Ms. Harakiri, Hungry Beast (panda cyborg) | Class identity OK; carried into Operation Galuga 6-character roster |
| Weapon overheating | Press-to-fire with cooldown meter; over-fire = lockout | NOT carried forward — replaced by ammo-pickup model |
| Twin-stick controls | Move L stick, aim R stick, fire R2, dash L2, jump X, swap Square, special R1, bomb Triangle | Twin-stick paradigm rejected for the franchise |

## Why It Failed

Multiple reviews cite "twin-stick + cooldown + RPG-lite + 3D camera" as too many simultaneous deviations from the 1987 baseline. Confirms the [[Contra Per Entry Mechanic Matrix]] survival rule: **changes that alter perspective + change weapon timing + change input scheme simultaneously fail**.
