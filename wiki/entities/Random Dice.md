# Random Dice

## Identity

- **Full title:** Random Dice: PvP Defense (also "Random Dice Defense: PvP TD")
- **Developer/Publisher:** 111% (Korean studio)
- **Year:** 2019
- **Platforms:** iOS, Android, PC (BlueStacks)
- **Genre:** PvP lane defense / merge tower defense

## Why It Matters

Random Dice is the **modern Korean exemplar** of lane defense and the most successful PvP-first lane defense title to date. It introduced the **merge dice mechanic** as a replacement for tower placement, fundamentally rethinking what the strategic surface of lane defense can be.

For a Korean indie team studying line defense, Random Dice is mandatory reference reading. It demonstrates that:

1. Lane defense scales to symmetric PvP.
2. Merge mechanics can replace placement decisions entirely.
3. Mobile session length (3–7 minutes per match) is a viable target for the genre.

## Mechanical Profile

(Source: [[Random Dice App Store]], [[Random Dice BlueStacks]])

- **Pre-match loadout:** Choose 5 dice types from a large pool.
- **In-match action:** Press Roll to spawn a random die from the deck onto the board.
- **Merge:** Two same-type same-pip dice combine into one die at pip+1.
- **Wave shared:** Both players face identical wave; loser is whoever's HP hits zero first.
- **Match length:** ~5 minutes typical.

## Game Modes

- **Real-time PvP** — global matchmaking, ladder ranks
- **Co-op Boss Raid** — cooperative wave defense
- **Solo mode** — PvE progression
- **Crew Battles** — guild-vs-guild
- **Mirror mode** — both players use the same deck (skill-pure ladder)
- **Season ranking events** — limited-time competitive modes

## Meta Layer

Heavy meta progression by genre standards:

- **Card Power level** — global stat boost per dice type, fueled by duplicate cards.
- **Class level** — gates higher PvP arenas.
- **Crew system** — guilds give passive bonuses.
- **Battlepass / season rewards** — F2P retention loop.

This is the most predatory dimension of the design and is widely flagged in player reviews. The ladder is heavily P2W at top tiers.

## Lessons For This Project

- **Merge is genre-defining when you commit to it.** Half-merge designs feel undercooked.
- **PvP demands minimum-diversity guarantees.** Random Dice's dice rolls have hidden anti-streak logic (player reverse-engineering suggests so, though specifics aren't public).
- **Korean market accepts deeper meta than Western indie market.** Calibrate accordingly.
- **Mirror mode is a brilliant idea** — lets devs demonstrate balance integrity. Worth implementing in any PvP system.

## Failure Modes Observed

- **Card-level treadmill.** New players cannot win at intermediate ranks without grinding card levels. The skill-vs-meta balance leans hard toward meta.
- **Synergy combinatorics.** With 60+ dice types in 5-slot decks, many combos are unbalanced. Patches are constant.
