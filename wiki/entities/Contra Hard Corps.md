# Contra: Hard Corps (1994)

Sega Genesis run-and-gun by Konami. The first Contra entry to introduce **branching narrative**, **character select**, and **vehicle stages** as differentiating systems on top of the 1987 base loop.

## Differentiated Systems

| System | Detail | Survived To |
|---|---|---|
| Branching paths | 4 routes / 5 endings via mid-game choice points | Hard Corps: Uprising |
| Character select | 4 characters with unique special moves (Browny hover, Fang claw) | Operation Galuga, Rogue Corps |
| Sliding | Universal i-frame slide, debut in series | Operation Galuga (return) |
| 4+1 weapon slots | 4 special weapon slots + 1 bomb slot | (one-time inventory size) |
| Motoroid hoverbike | Transformable robot vehicle stage | Contra III lineage |

## Notes

All stages retain side-view perspective — unlike Contra III which used Mode 7 top-down, Hard Corps deliberately stayed 2D side-scroller throughout.
