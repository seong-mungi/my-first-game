# Campaign Asia: Battle Cats Player Recovery

## Reference

- **URL:** https://www.campaignasia.com/article/how-ponos-battle-cats-clawed-back-more-than-a-million-lost-players/498928
- **Type:** Marketing industry press (Campaign Asia)
- **Reliability:** High for marketing/retention data; campaign metrics from agency RGA

## Key Claims Extracted

### Churn Cause
- 1.2 million lapsed players identified
- Complexity barrier: 1,000+ characters intimidated casual players
- Narrative shifted from "easy to pick up" to increasingly complex at advanced levels

### Recovery Strategy (Agency: RGA)
- Community voting system: players voted for favorite units ($0.99/vote)
- Categories: "best attacker," "best defender" — winners unlocked in-game rewards
- 20 character categories × diverse formats: OOH ads, digital, influencer, video, social
- Offline event in Korea: live celebration gathering, tickets became "badge of honour"

### Results
- **Korea:** DAU +137%, Revenue +207%, New Users +145%, Returning Users +129.8%
- **Taiwan:** DAU +119%, Revenue +144%, New Users +127%, Returning Users +128%
- 1 million+ lapsed players recovered
- Exceeded all acquisition targets

## Key Insight

Community ownership (voting with real stakes) outperformed traditional discount/re-engagement campaigns. The game's complexity was not simplified — it was weaponized as a community asset.
