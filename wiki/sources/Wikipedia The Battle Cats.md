## Key Claims Extracted

- Developer: PONOS Corporation, Kyoto, Japan (founded 1990)
- Japan iOS release: November 15, 2012; Worldwide: September 17, 2014
- Genre: Free-to-play tower defense
- Platforms: iOS, Android, Nintendo 3DS (2016), Switch (2018–2021), Windows (2017–2018)
- Story chapters: Empire of Cats, Into the Future, Cats of the Cosmos, The Aku Realms; plus Legend Stages
- 144+ stages across main sagas
- Monetization: Cat Capsules (gacha) using Cat Food (premium currency) and tickets
- Cumulative worldwide revenue through June 2024: $700 million (~102 billion yen) — Sensor Tower estimate
- PONOS became Williams F1 team partner for 2020–2021 seasons
- Expanded to 3DS, Switch, Windows alongside mobile
